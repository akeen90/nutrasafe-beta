Calorie Counter Questions

Is NutraSafe calorie counter free?

Yes, NutraSafe offers a free calorie counter with barcode scanning and access to our UK food database. Free users can log up to 5 foods per day ‚Äî plenty for light tracking. Premium unlocks unlimited daily logging, detailed additive analysis, and pattern recognition from ¬£2.99/month.

How many UK foods are in the database?

NutraSafe has over 500,000 UK foods including products from Tesco, Asda, Sainsbury's, Morrisons, Aldi, Lidl, M&S, Waitrose, Co-op, and more. We also include restaurant meals from Greggs, Nando's, Costa, Pret, McDonald's, and other UK chains. Our database is updated regularly with new products.

Can I track macros with NutraSafe?

Yes, NutraSafe tracks all macronutrients including protein, carbohydrates, fat, fibre, and sugar. You can set personalised macro goals (like 40/30/30 split) and see your daily progress in real-time. Perfect for keto, low-carb, high-protein, or any other macro-focused diet.

How accurate is the calorie counting?

NutraSafe uses verified UK food data directly from product labels and official sources. Our barcode scanner pulls exact nutritional information from manufacturer data. For best accuracy, we recommend weighing portions and logging immediately after eating. UK food labels are legally required to be accurate within 20%.

What makes NutraSafe different from other calorie counters?

NutraSafe is built specifically for UK users with a comprehensive British food database. Unlike US-focused apps, we include UK supermarket own-brands, British restaurant chains, and use UK portion sizes. Plus, we offer unique features like food additive warnings, reaction tracking, and expiry date monitoring that other calorie counters don't have.
