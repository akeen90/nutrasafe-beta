How to Track Food Effectively

Getting the most from food tracking is straightforward with NutraSafe:

Food Tracker FAQs

What is a food tracker?
A food tracker is a tool for logging everything you eat and drink. It calculates calories, protein, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins and minerals from your meals. Food trackers help you understand your eating patterns, meet nutrition goals, and make healthier choices.
How do I track my food intake?
With NutraSafe, you can track food by scanning barcodes, searching our UK food database, or taking photos of meals. The tracker automatically calculates nutritional values and adds them to your daily totals. You can log breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks throughout the day.
Is food tracking effective for weight management?
Yes, research shows that people who track their food intake are more successful at managing their weight. Food tracking creates awareness of what you're eating, helps identify patterns, and makes it easier to stay within calorie targets or meet specific nutritional goals.
Can I track vitamins and micronutrients?
Yes. NutraSafe tracks all major vitamins (A, B vitamins, C, D, E, K) and micronutrients including iron, calcium, magnesium, zinc, potassium, and more. You can see daily percentages and identify nutrients you may be lacking.
Does the food tracker work with UK foods?
Yes. NutraSafe includes an extensive database of UK supermarket products from Tesco, Sainsbury's, Asda, Morrisons, Aldi, Lidl, Waitrose and more. It also includes generic foods, restaurant meals and international products.
Is the food tracker free?
Yes, basic food tracking is free. You can log up to 5 foods per day, scan barcodes, and view your daily nutritional intake at no cost. Premium unlocks unlimited daily logging and additional insights.
