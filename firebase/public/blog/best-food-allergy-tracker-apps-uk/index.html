Compare the best food allergy tracking apps for UK users. Features, costs, barcode scanning, instant allergen warnings, and symptom logging reviewed.

Best Food Allergy Tracker Apps for UK (2026)

1. NutraSafe — Best Overall

Instant allergen warnings, UK database, vitamin tracking

  • ✓ Barcode scanning with instant allergen alerts
  • ✓ 14 common allergens tracked (nuts, dairy, gluten, soy, etc.)
  • ✓ 5 free scans per day
  • ✓ Tracks symptoms and food reactions
  • ✓ Comprehensive UK supermarket database
  • ✓ E-number additive warnings

Cost: Free with 5 scans/day. £2.99/month unlimited.

2. Spoon Guru — Best for Multiple Allergies

Personalised product recommendations, UK supermarkets

  • ✓ Set up complex allergy profiles
  • ✓ Integration with Tesco, Sainsbury's, Ocado
  • ✓ Recipe suggestions matching your allergies
  • ✗ Doesn't track daily intake
  • ✗ Limited free features

Cost: Free basic, £3.99/month premium.

3. MyFitnessPal — Most Popular

Huge database, allergen filtering

  • ✓ Massive food database
  • ✓ Can exclude allergen-containing foods
  • ✗ Barcode scanner behind paywall (£19.99/month)
  • ✗ No instant allergen warnings
  • ✗ Heavy ads on free version

Key Features to Look For

  • Barcode scanning: Instant ingredient checks while shopping
  • Allergen database: Must cover all 14 UK allergens
  • Symptom tracking: Log reactions to identify triggers
  • UK focus: Accurate database for British supermarkets
  • Customizable alerts: Set your specific allergens

Who Should Use Which App?

Choose NutraSafe if: You want instant allergen warnings while shopping, track nutrition beyond allergies, and need barcode scanning on free tier.

Choose Spoon Guru if: You have multiple complex allergies and want recipe/product recommendations integrated with online supermarkets.

Choose MyFitnessPal if: You're willing to pay £19.99/month for premium and want the largest food database.
