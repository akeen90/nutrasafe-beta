## Content Template for New Pages

Each page should include:
1. **H1** with primary keyword
2. **Introduction** (100-150 words) with keyword in first paragraph
3. **Table of contents** for long pages
4. **Main content sections** with H2s containing secondary keywords
5. **Feature highlights** with icons/visuals
6. **Comparison section** (if applicable)
7. **FAQ section** (5-10 questions with FAQ schema)
8. **CTA section** with App Store link
9. **Related pages** section with internal links
