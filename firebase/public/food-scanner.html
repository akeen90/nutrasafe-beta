Why Use a Food Scanner?

Understanding food labels can be challenging. Ingredient lists are often long and confusing, with chemical names and E-numbers that mean nothing to most people. A food scanner cuts through the complexity.

For People with Allergies

Set your allergens in the app once, and every scan will immediately warn you if a product contains something you need to avoid. No more squinting at tiny labels in the supermarket aisle.

For Health-Conscious Shoppers

See beyond marketing claims. Our scanner shows you the actual nutritional content, processing level, and additive safety ratings so you can make choices based on facts, not packaging.

For Families

Quickly check products before putting them in your trolley. Know exactly what additives and E-numbers you're feeding your family and make informed decisions about what to buy.

Food Scanner FAQs

What is a food scanner?

A food scanner is a mobile tool that reads barcodes on food packaging to instantly display nutritional information, ingredients, additives, allergens, and health ratings. Simply point your phone camera at any barcode and get detailed information about what's in your food.

How does a food scanner work?

Food scanners use your phone's camera to read the barcode on food packaging. The barcode number is matched against a database of products to retrieve nutritional data, ingredients, additives (E-numbers), allergen warnings, and health scores. Results appear instantly on your screen.

Which UK supermarket products can I scan?

NutraSafe's food scanner works with products from all major UK supermarkets including Tesco, Sainsbury's, Asda, Morrisons, Aldi, Lidl, Waitrose, Co-op, M&S, and more. Our database covers thousands of UK products.

Is the food scanner free to use?

Yes, NutraSafe's food scanner is free to download and use. You can scan barcodes and view nutritional information at no cost. Free users can log up to 5 foods per day to their diary. Premium unlocks unlimited logging, detailed additive analysis, and pattern tracking.
