Frequently Asked Questions

What is NutraSafe and how does it work? ‚ñº

NutraSafe is a comprehensive food intelligence app that helps you understand what you're eating. Simply scan any food barcode with your phone's camera, and instantly receive detailed information about ingredients, additives, allergens, nutritional content, and health impacts. Our database covers hundreds of thousands of products and includes scientific research on food additives and processing methods.

How are the health scores calculated? ‚ñº

Our health scores are calculated using a proprietary algorithm that considers multiple factors: processing level, additive safety profiles based on scientific studies, nutritional density, presence of allergens, and content of beneficial vs. harmful ingredients. Each factor is weighted based on current nutritional science and public health recommendations.

Which products can I scan with NutraSafe? ‚ñº

You can scan any packaged food or beverage product with a barcode. Our database includes products from major supermarkets, specialty stores, and international brands. If a product isn't in our database, you can request it to be added, and our team will process it within 24-48 hours.

What if a product doesn't exist in the database? ‚ñº

If you scan a product that's not in our database, you'll have the option to submit it for review. Our team and AI systems will analyze the product and add it to the database, usually within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification when the product information is available.

Is NutraSafe free to use? ‚ñº

NutraSafe offers a free tier with unlimited barcode scanning and basic nutritional information. Premium features like detailed additive analysis, pattern recognition for intolerances, and advanced nutrition tracking are available through our subscription plans starting at ¬£1.99/month.
