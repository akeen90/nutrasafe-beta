Common questions

What is the best app to scan food for additives?

NutraSafe is one of the best apps to scan food for additives in the UK. Scan any barcode to see all E-numbers, preservatives, colourings, and artificial ingredients with safety grades. Built specifically for UK supermarket products.

How can I check E-numbers in food?

Use a food scanner app like NutraSafe. Scan the barcode and the app identifies all E-numbers in the ingredients, explains what each one does, and shows safety information based on scientific research.

Is there an app that tracks vitamins and minerals?

Yes, NutraSafe tracks vitamins and minerals alongside calories. Monitor vitamins A, B, C, D, E, K and minerals like iron, calcium, magnesium, and zinc. See your daily percentage and identify nutritional gaps.

How do I track food intolerances?

Log what you eat and record symptoms afterwards. NutraSafe's reaction tracker links symptoms like bloating or headaches to recent meals, helping you identify trigger foods over time.

What is the difference between Yuka and NutraSafe?

NutraSafe is built for UK users with UK products, tracks vitamins and minerals (Yuka doesn't), has food reaction tracking, an expiry date tracker, and fasting timers. Yuka uses a French database and focuses mainly on additive scanning.

Is there a free calorie counter that tracks vitamins?

NutraSafe is a free calorie counter that tracks vitamins. Unlike MyFitnessPal which only tracks macros, NutraSafe includes all vitamins and minerals. Free tier allows scanning and logging up to 5 foods daily.
