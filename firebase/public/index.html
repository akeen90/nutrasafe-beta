FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is NutraSafe and how does it work?

NutraSafe is a comprehensive food intelligence app that helps you understand what you're eating. Simply scan any food barcode with your phone's camera, and instantly receive detailed information about ingredients, additives, allergens, nutritional content, and health impacts. Our database covers hundreds of thousands of products.

How are the health scores calculated?

Our health scores consider multiple factors: processing level, additive safety profiles based on scientific studies, nutritional density, presence of allergens, and content of beneficial vs. harmful ingredients. Each factor is weighted based on current nutritional science.

Which products can I scan?

You can scan any packaged food or beverage product with a barcode. Our database includes products from major UK supermarkets, specialty stores, and international brands. If a product isn't in our database, you can request it to be added.

Can I check food additives and E-numbers?

Yes! NutraSafe scans barcodes and instantly shows you all food additives and E-numbers in any product. Our comprehensive database identifies potentially harmful additives, ultra-processed ingredients, and provides safety information based on scientific research. Perfect for checking E-numbers in UK food products.

Does NutraSafe track vitamins and micronutrients?

Absolutely. NutraSafe tracks all major vitamins (A, B, C, D, E, K) and micronutrients including iron, calcium, magnesium, zinc, selenium and more. You can monitor your daily vitamin intake and see which nutrients you're meeting or missing throughout the day and week.

Can I use NutraSafe for intermittent fasting?

Yes! NutraSafe includes a built-in intermittent fasting timer. Track your fasting windows for 16:8, 18:6, or custom fasting schedules. The app helps you monitor eating windows and fasting periods throughout the day.

How does the food expiry tracker work?

Add items to your use-by tracker when you open them or bring food home. Set custom expiry dates and NutraSafe will send you reminders before food expires. Track opened packets, leftovers, and perishables to reduce waste and stay safe. Perfect for managing your fridge and cupboard inventory.

Is NutraSafe free to use?

NutraSafe offers a free tier with unlimited barcode scanning and basic nutritional information. Premium features like detailed additive analysis, pattern recognition for intolerances, and advanced nutrition tracking are available through our subscription plans starting at ¬£2.99/month.
