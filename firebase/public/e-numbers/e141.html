What is E141 (Copper Chlorophyllin)?

E141 is Copper Chlorophyllin, a semi-synthetic green pigment made from natural chlorophyll.

How it's made:

  • Extracted from plants ‚Äì grass, nettles, alfalfa
  • Treated with copper salts ‚Äì replaces magnesium with copper
  • Result: More stable, brighter green colour

Why it's used:

  • Stable green colour ‚Äì doesn't fade like pure chlorophyll
  • Natural origin ‚Äì appeals to "clean label" trend
  • Doesn't change with pH ‚Äì unlike some colours

E140 vs E141

  • E141 (Copper Chlorophyllin): Copper-modified ‚Äì more stable, brighter

E141 is preferred by manufacturers because it maintains its green colour better during processing and storage.
