Health Benefits

Sodium bicarbonate (E500ii) has medical uses:

1. Heartburn and Indigestion Relief

  • Neutralizes stomach acid
  • Active ingredient in many antacids

2. Athletic Performance

  • May buffer lactic acid during intense exercise
  • Some athletes take it as a supplement

3. Kidney Health

  • Used medically to treat metabolic acidosis
  • Prescribed for some kidney conditions
