Why Choose NutraSafe Over Yuka?

Yuka is excellent for basic product scoring but has significant limitations for UK users:

  • No allergen detection: Yuka doesn't flag UK allergens, which is critical for people with allergies or intolerances.
  • Limited UK products: Yuka's database prioritises French and European products. Many UK supermarket own-brands aren't recognised.
  • No nutrition tracking: Yuka only rates products but doesn't let you log meals or track your daily nutrition intake.
  • Expensive premium: Yuka Premium costs £12.99/month — over 4x more than NutraSafe for fewer features.

Why Choose NutraSafe Over MyFitnessPal?

MyFitnessPal dominates calorie counting but lacks food safety features:

  • No additive warnings: MyFitnessPal shows calories and macros but completely ignores E-numbers, additives, and ultra-processed ingredients.
  • No allergen detection: You'll need to manually read ingredient lists to check for allergens.
  • Ad-heavy free version: MyFitnessPal free version shows frequent ads. NutraSafe is completely ad-free.
  • US-centric database: While MyFitnessPal has UK foods, it's built for American users with US portion sizes and less accurate UK supermarket data.

Why UK Users Choose NutraSafe

Over 10,000 UK users have downloaded NutraSafe to make safer, healthier food choices. Here's what makes NutraSafe the best food scanner for British shoppers:

Unlike US-focused apps like MyFitnessPal or French apps like Yuka, NutraSafe is designed from the ground up for UK users. We use British spelling (colour, fibre, sulphites), understand UK portion sizes (pints, stones, serving sizes from UK labels), and prioritise UK supermarket products.

Many people suspect certain foods cause problems (bloating, headaches, skin issues, fatigue) but don't know which ones. NutraSafe's unique reaction diary lets you:

This feature is invaluable for people exploring elimination diets, managing IBS, or investigating food intolerances without expensive allergy tests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is NutraSafe completely free?

NutraSafe offers a generous free tier with unlimited barcode scanning, allergen detection, E-number analysis, and basic nutrition tracking (5 food logs per day). Premium (£2.99/month) unlocks unlimited daily food logging, detailed additive reports, pattern recognition in reaction tracking, and advanced micronutrient analysis.

Does NutraSafe work offline?

You need internet connection for the initial barcode scan to retrieve product data. Once scanned, products are cached locally so you can view them offline. Your food diary and reaction logs are stored locally and sync when you're back online.

How accurate is the allergen detection?

NutraSafe pulls allergen information directly from product packaging and manufacturer databases. We verify data against official UK allergen labelling requirements. However, manufacturing processes change, so always double-check the physical package label for the most current information, especially for severe allergies.

What if a product isn't in your database?

If NutraSafe doesn't recognise a barcode, you can take a photo of the nutrition label and our AI will extract the information instantly. You can also manually add products. We're constantly adding new UK products to our database based on user scans.

Can I use NutraSafe for weight loss?

Absolutely. NutraSafe combines calorie counting, macro tracking, and barcode scanning. Set your weight loss goal and daily calorie target, then scan and log everything you eat. Unlike other apps, you'll also see food quality (UPF scores, additive warnings) alongside calorie counts.

Does it work with recipe ingredients?

Yes. You can scan individual ingredients when cooking from scratch. NutraSafe will calculate total nutrition for your homemade meals. You can save custom recipes and portion them out for accurate meal logging.

How does NutraSafe compare to Yuka?

NutraSafe is built specifically for UK users with comprehensive allergen detection (which Yuka lacks), nutrition tracking, and food reaction logging. Yuka excels at basic product rating but doesn't offer meal tracking or diet management. NutraSafe Premium is also £10/month cheaper than Yuka Premium (£2.99 vs £12.99). Can I track vitamins and minerals?

Yes. NutraSafe tracks all major vitamins (A, B-complex, C, D, E, K) and minerals (iron, calcium, magnesium, zinc, potassium, sodium) with daily percentage targets. Your food diary, reaction logs, and health data are stored securely and encrypted. We never sell your data to third parties. You can export or delete all your data at any time. NutraSafe is GDPR compliant and follows UK data protection regulations.

Does NutraSafe show E-numbers and additives?

Yes. Every product scan shows a complete list of E-numbers and food additives with plain-language explanations. We flag additives that may affect children's behaviour, contain allergens, or aren't suitable for vegan/halal diets. Learn more about E-numbers and food additives.
